Timeline:

1929- She was born in a Maharashtrian Brahmin Mangeshkar Family on 28 September 1929 in Indore, Madhya Pradesh.

1943- She sang her first Hindi song "Mata ek sapoot ki duniya badal de tu" at the age of 14 for the Marathi film Gajaabhaau

1949- Her break came in 1949 with the movie, "Mahal" for which she sang the tumultuously famous song, "Aayega Aanewala."

1958- She won her first filmfare award for the song, "Aaja Re Pardesi" in the movie, "Madhumati"

1963- She sang, "Aye Mere Watan Ke Logon" a song meant to inspire the people of the country after the Indo-China war

1973- She won her first National Film award for the song, "Beeti na bitai" in the movie, "Parichay"

1999- She was awarded the Padma Vibushan in 1999, the second-highest civilian award in India.

2001- She was awarded the Bharat Ratna, the highest civilian honor in India.

2019- Lata Mangeshkar recorded her last song "Saugandh Mujhe iss Mitti ki"- a tribute to Indian Army and the Nation

2022- On 8 January 2022, Lata Mangeshkar tested positive for COVID-19 with mild symptoms and was admitted to Breach Candy Hospital, Mumbai

2022- On 6 February 2022, Lata Mangeshkar died from Multiple Organ Dysfunction Syndrome at the age of 92
